### Q4. Rotating Question (varies by pulse)

| Pulse | Week | Question |
|-------|------|----------|
| Pulse 1 | Wk 2 | **Barriers to entry** — "Has anything been frustrating about using Claude so far? If so, what?" |
| Pulse 2 | Wk 4 | **Extensive margin/Task completion** — "Did Claude Code help you complete a task you would have otherwise abandoned, outsourced, or significantly delayed?" |
| Pulse 3 | Wk 6 | **Experimentation** — "Did Claude Code make you more willing to try a method or approach you wouldn't have attempted otherwise?" |
| Pulse 4 | Wk 8 | **Workflow stage** — "In which stage(s) of the research process was Claude Code most helpful?" |

**Rationale:**
- Pulse 1: Early barriers to entry -- we can potentially send materials to solve these 
- Pulse 2: By week 4, subjects have encountered real research bottlenecks
- Pulse 3: By week 6, enough familiarity to take risks
- Pulse 4: Final pulse — captures mature usage patterns
